Frequently Asked Questions

What is Location Satsang? All VISVAS students are enrolled in two Satsangs - Your batch satsang (e.g. A-64) and also a locational Satsang based in your residential location (e.g. Bangalore Visvas Satsang).

While both Satsangs are done on line only now, this will help in the group meeting together at a local temple or residence (once pandemic is over) and chanting VSN.
Would that mean 2 satsangs a week? Yes. One satsang as a part of A-Batch and one more from Locational Satsang perspective.
Will there be a separate coordinator for this? Yes. Decided based on location. You can also express your interest if you wish to be a coordinator.
Can the discussions happen in local language? e.g. if it is Kerala location, will they converse in Malayalam? Yes. That improves the closeness within the location. English also can be spoken.
Are these happening across India? Yes. To name few, East India, North India, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Telangana, Andhra, Kerala, Belagavi, depending upon density of our student devotees.
Where do I get my local language Vishnu Sahasranamam and Pledge? Right here: https://visvasdocs.github.io/
[for Coordinator] Is there any easy way of allocating shlokas? Right here: https://visvasdocs.github.io/visvasallocate.html. Fill the names here one below the other and hit allocate button. It happens in a second!
